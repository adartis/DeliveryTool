Application Instructions

Starting Your Project

To begin, open the application and select "New Project" to initialise a new project workspace. This will create a space where you can add, edit, and organise your data.

Filling in Forms

In each section, you'll find forms with various fields to enter project details. Carefully fill in each field as needed, as these details will be essential to your project overview.

Be mindful of mandatory fields and any prompts within the application that indicate specific input requirements.

Updating with 'Save'

After entering or editing data, click the 'Save' button to apply your changes. This action will store your updates in the current session and will reflect across the application in real-time.

Remember to save regularly to ensure all modifications are kept up-to-date within the project space.
